About

Welcome to your guide to an exciting two year AP degree that combines design, business, coding and communication and offers you the chance to specialise in electives and study abroad. An Academy Profession programme is a higher education with a duration of 2 years, that consists of four semesters which add up to 120 ECTS points. KEA places great emphasis on project work, on contact with the business community and on use of the latest technologies. During the two years students will gain access to a variety of teaching methods, online tutorials and gain practical experience that will provide the next generation of multimedia designers with the knowledge and interpersonal skills needed to take on this faced moving industry.

Innovation

We encourage an active learning community and an environment that nurtures creative ideas that can be used to create digital innovation. Our methods of teaching are dynamic and forward looking.The instruction is based on projects, which provide a basis for understanding practice, and the tasks are normally carried out in groups. Classroom instruction is gradually being replaced by advisor-based instruction. KEA is in constant dialogue with companies and industry associations to keep education programmes updated in both form and content. This ensures that the education meets the current requirements and needs of a dynamic labour market. You are therefore well prepared when choosing a future with KEA.

KEA Connect

KEA Connect is KEAs big combined info meeting, open house and student expo event for all programmes held twice a year. Would you like to get a close connection with business life - working within the field of DESIGN, TECH or DIGITAL? Then come to KEA CONNECT to get a glimpse of the KEA life and talk to students and study counsellors from KEA. All of KEA's programmes are represented and you can meet KEA graduates who will talk about how they use their education in an exciting work life in design or IT. You can also see examples of student works from their semester projects which are on display.

Entrepreneurship

Multimedia Design graduates leave with the skills to take on their own projects and develop them from the ground up. With a strong network of other students with a variety of skills.
