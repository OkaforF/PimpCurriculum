About

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts. Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the Semantics, a large language ocean. A small river named Duden flows by their place and supplies it with the necessary regelialia. It is a paradisematic country, in which roasted parts of sentences fly into your mouth. Even the all-powerful Pointing has no control about the blind texts it is an almost unorthographic life One day however a small line of blind text by the name of Lorem Ipsum decided to leave for the far World of Grammar. The Big Oxmox advised her not to do so, because there were thousands of bad Commas, wild Question Marks and devious Semikoli, but the Little Blind Text didn’t listen.

Internships

At KEA our apprach to learning is that students should not only know but be able to. Because of this practical approach, students spend ten weeks during the fourth semster undertaking a semester either in a Danish company or abroad. During their internship they have the chance to use the skills they learned in a professional environment and working on real projects which are evaluated at the end of the internship.

Study Abroad

KEA prides itself on it’s global outlook and after passing the first year exam, students can choose to go abroad for their 3rd semester or the entire programme at one one our partner institutions in Europe or further afield. The equivalent study programmes are assessed according to the grading system at the institution where the exam is held.

Career & Top Up

At the end of the AP degree students have the option od going on to careers in art direction, User Interface design and web development. Students also qualify for entry to a top-up degree where they can earn a BA degree. The top-up takes 1 1/2 year and consists on three semester which adds up to 90 ECTS points.
